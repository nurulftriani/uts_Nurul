Our Profile

Cimory exists to make every day fresher, healthier, and happier — from the farm to your table

Cimory (Cisarua Mountain Dairy) is an Indonesian dairy company established with a simple mission to bring fresh, high-quality dairy products made from 100% real milk directly from local farms to families across Indonesia. Since its founding in 2006, Cimory has grown into one of the most trusted and loved dairy brands in the country, known for its freshness, delicious taste, and commitment to quality.
